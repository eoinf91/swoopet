About Swoopet

Swoopet are committed to creating practical and innovative products which will enhance the health and wellbeing of your community environment, your family, and your pets.

We all love our pets, but nobody likes cleaning up after them! Swoopet was designed by an Engineer and dog-lover, who identified a need for an efficient, hygienic and convenient waste management solution to use when walking his dogs. He wanted to keep his family safe from the many illnesses which exposure to a pet’s waste may cause. Like many of us, he regards his pets as members of the family, and his family’s health was paramount in the design and development of the Swoopet products.

Innovation, Design & Quality are our core values, inspiring us to create new and innovative products which will revolutionize the way you feel about cleaning up after the pets you love. Not found anywhere else globally, the innovative and patented Swoopet products are also affordably priced and built to last. Customers can be assured that all our products are manufactured to the highest quality and ethical standards, and comply with UKAS, IAF and ISO 9001 standards. Our objective is to use premium materials which comply with the Consumer Product Standards, Supply chain efficiencies and material testing.

User Benefits

  • The Swoopet is small, lightweight and compact.
  • Swoopet is designed to form part of the dog leash, conveniently at hand when walking your pet.
  • Swoopet holds multiple (on average 20) dog waste bags within it’s storage and dispensing chamber.
  • Swoopet easily and hygienically picks up dog waste from most surfaces under your pet’s feet. ie. concrete, grass, tar macadam, brick pavers, gravel, snow and ice surfaces and uneven surfaces.
  • Swoopet is suitable for dogs of all sizes.
  • Swoopet can pick up multiple dog wastes in one bag.
  • Swoopet removes the sensation of feeling your pet’s warm waste through a bag, making the collection process significantly more bearable. It’s the no fuss, no feel solution!
  • A used dog waste bag holder has been built into the Swoopet Retractable, Swoopet Leash Link and the Swoopet Bag Dispenser, so the used waste bag can be hygienically stored until the waste can be properly disposed of into a waste disposal bin.
  • Swoopet is easy to clean and maintain; just rinse under a hot tap after use, and allow to air dry.
  • Swoopet is designed to be used comfortably by people of all ages capable of managing a pet on a leash.
  • Swoopet is made from durable material, tested extensively at the design stage, and is built to last.
