Usage Instructions

Pet Safety First

You’ll need both hands to use Swoopet. Wear the wrist strap to retain control of your pet while operating Swoopet.

Getting Started

Grip the Swoopet with your hand as shown, expand your fingers and thumb to fully extend the Swoopet blades for larger waste.

Feed the first bag from the roll of bags through the dispensing slot, and push the remaining roll into the chamber. Refit the roll winder and turn the outer screw to lock.

Place the Swoopet into the bag, blades first, then press the bag between the blades with your fingers as shown.

User Benefits

  • The Swoopet is small, lightweight and compact.
  • Swoopet is designed to form part of the dog leash, conveniently at hand when walking your pet.
  • Swoopet holds multiple (on average 20) dog waste bags within it’s storage and dispensing chamber.
  • Swoopet easily and hygienically picks up dog waste from most surfaces under your pet’s feet. ie. concrete, grass, tar macadam, brick pavers, gravel, snow and ice surfaces and uneven surfaces.
  • Swoopet is suitable for dogs of all sizes.
  • Swoopet can pick up multiple dog wastes in one bag.
  • Swoopet removes the sensation of feeling your pet’s warm waste through a bag, making the collection process significantly more bearable. It’s the no fuss, no feel solution!
  • A used dog waste bag holder has been built into the Swoopet Retractable, Swoopet Leash Link and the Swoopet Bag Dispenser, so the used waste bag can be hygienically stored until the waste can be properly disposed of into a waste disposal bin.
  • Swoopet is easy to clean and maintain; just rinse under a hot tap after use, and allow to air dry.
  • Swoopet is designed to be used comfortably by people of all ages capable of managing a pet on a leash.
  • Swoopet is made from durable material, tested extensively at the design stage, and is built to last.

Health & Safety Precautions

  • Keep away from children, this product is not a toy, and contains small components and including plastic bags, which are dangerous to children, and may cause choking or suffocation.
  • Examine your Swoopet Retractable before every use, as all products can be subject to wear and tear.
  • Pet Safety First, You’ll need both hands to use Swoopet, so wear the wrist strap to retain control of your pet while operating Swoopet.
  • Never allow children to use your Swoopet Retractable.
  • Ensure to always firmly grip the handle of your leash, when walking your dog. Your wrist strap is solely provided to ensure you keep control of your dog while picking up your pet’s waste.
  • If your Swoopet leash tape gets wet, pull the wet tape from it’s casing, then activate the locking mechanism, and allow to dry.
  • If the leash tape gets damaged in any way, which maybe from bites, do not continue to use your Swoopet Retractable Lead
  • Ensure to activate the locking mechanism to limit your dog’s leash distance. If you wish your dog to walk close by your side, shorten the leash length, and lock in position.
  • Do not use Swoopet Retractable Lead to tie your dog to a post or other anchor.
  • Do not grasp the leash tape with your hand, always allow the mechanical mechanism to retract the length of the tape.
  • Do not wrap or wind the leash tape around any part of your body, as it may cause injury.
  • Regularly check that your pet’s collar or harness for weakness over time, before linking your Swoopet retractable when taking you pet for a walk.
  • To heel your dog, shorten the length of your leash tape and active the locking mechanism for the duration of the walk.
  • Never open the Swoopet Retractable casing, for risk of injury due to recoiling of the tension spring.
