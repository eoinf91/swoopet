Usage Instructions

Pet Safety First

You’ll need both hands to use Swoopet. Wear the wrist strap to retain control of your pet while operating Swoopet.

To Use

Step 01

Feed the first bag from the roll of bags through the dispensing slot and push the remaining roll into the chamber. Refit the roll winder and turn outer screw to lock.

Step 02

Push & Turn the central roll winder to retract any extended bags.

Health & Safety Precautions

  • Keep away from children, this product is not a toy, and contains small components and including plastic bags, which are dangerous to children, and may cause choking or suffocation
  • Examine your Swoopet Bag Dispenser before every use, as all products can be subject to wear and tear
  • Ensure that the Velcro strap is properly linked and closed to your rope, nylon or retractable leashes available on the market
  • Never allow children to use your Swoopet Bag Dispenser
  • Ensure to always firmly grip the handle of your rope, nylon or retractable leash when walking your dog
  • Regularly check that your pet’s collar or harness for weaknesses over time, before taking your pet for a walk
