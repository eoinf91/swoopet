Usage Instructions

Pet Safety First

Ensure to keep a firm grip of the leash at all times while operating Swoopet. Lock the tape or chord length for greater control.

Getting Started

Step A

Grip the Swoopet with your hand as shown. Expand your fingers and thumb to fully extend the Swoopet blades for larger wastes.

Step B

Feed the first bag from the roll of bags through the dispensing slot and push the remaining roll into the chamber. Refit the roll winder and turn outer screw to lock.

Step C

Push & Turn the central roll winder to retract any extended bags.

To Use

Step 01

Place the Swoopet into the bag, blades first, then pushing the bag between the blades with your fingers as shown.

Step 02

Push the expanded bagged Swoopet into your pet's waste, squeeze and pick up, repeat if required.

Step 03

Remove the used bag, tie and place onto the used bag clip.

Health & Safety Precautions

  • Keep away from children, this product is not a toy, and contains small components and including plastic bags, which are dangerous to children, and may cause choking or suffocation
  • Examine your Swoopet Retractable before every use, as all products can be subject to wear and tear
  • Pet Safety First: Be sure to keep a firm grip of the leash at all times while operating Swoopet. Lock the tape length for greater control
  • Never allow children to use your Swoopet Retractable
  • Always firmly grip the handle of your leash, when walking your dog
  • If your Swoopet leash tape gets wet, pull the wet tape from it’s casing, then activate the locking mechanism, and allow to dry
  • If the leash tape gets damaged in any way, possibly from bites, do not continue to use your Swoopet Retractable Leash
  • Ensure to activate the locking mechanism to limit your dog’s leash distance. If you wish your dog to walk close by your side, shorten the tape length, and lock in position
  • Do not use Swoopet Retractable Leash to tie your dog to a post or other anchor
  • Do not grasp the leash tape with your hand, always allow the mechanical mechanism to retract the length of the tape
  • Do not wrap or wind the leash tape around any part of your body, as it may cause injury
  • Regularly check that your pet’s collar or harness for weakness over time, before linking your Swoopet retractable when taking your pet for a walk
  • To heel your dog, shorten the length of your leash tape and active the locking mechanism for the duration of the walk
  • Never open the Swoopet Retractable casing, for risk of injury due to recoiling of the tension spring
